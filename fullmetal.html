Fullmetal Alchemist

Fullmetal Alchemist is a manga and anime series created by Hiromu Arakawa. It is set in a world where alchemy, a scientific practice that can transform matter, is widely practiced. The story revolves around two brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric, who are skilled alchemists.
The series follows the Elric brothers' journey to find the Philosopher's Stone, a legendary artifact that is said to amplify alchemical powers and grant immortality. Their quest stems from an attempt to resurrect their deceased mother using forbidden alchemy, which resulted in severe consequences. Edward lost his left leg, and Alphonse's entire body was taken away, with his soul attached to a suit of armor.
Equipped with automail prosthetics and their alchemical knowledge, Edward and Alphonse join the State Military as State Alchemists in hopes of gaining access to resources and information that will aid them in finding the Philosopher's Stone. Along the way, they uncover a deep conspiracy and face powerful enemies, all while grappling with the ethical implications of their actions.
Fullmetal Alchemist explores themes of sacrifice, the consequences of playing god, the nature of humanity, and the pursuit of knowledge and redemption. It also delves into political and societal issues, as the Elric brothers navigate a world torn by war and corruption. There are two anime adaptations of Fullmetal Alchemist: Fullmetal Alchemist (2003) and Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood (2009). Fullmetal Alchemist (2003) was created while the manga was ongoing and diverges from the manga's storyline. It explores an alternative ending to the series. Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood, on the other hand, closely follows the original manga's plot and provides a more faithful adaptation of the complete story.
Both anime series have been widely acclaimed for their compelling storytelling, well-developed characters, and thought-provoking themes. They showcase a blend of action, drama, and humor while delving into the emotional journeys of the Elric brothers and their allies.
Fullmetal Alchemist has garnered a significant fanbase and is regarded as one of the greatest anime and manga series of all time. It has also inspired movies, spin-off manga, video games, and other adaptations, solidifying its place as a beloved and influential franchise.
